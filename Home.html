Approach

I was trainined in therapeutic evidence-based approaches, including:
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Dialetical Behavior Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and exposure and response prevention (ERP). 

I flexibly draw from these different approaches to meet the needs of each client. Therapy is a collaborative process where we work together to understand what you're dealing with and come up with a plan to help. You can also get a sense of my approach by reading the articles I've written to explain mental health.

I am warm, non-judgmental, naturally curious about people and what makes each person tick and well-trained to help you better understand yourself, and how to better manage difficult emotions, thoughts and behaviors. 

I can assist with a range of mental health and emotional issues. I specialize in treating depression, mood, and anxiety (generalized, social, OCD, panic) disorders. I can also help with "overthinking," such as rumination or worry as well as helping stop cycles that push us to engage in unhelpful behaviors.
