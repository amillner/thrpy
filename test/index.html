Get In Touch

Evidence-Based Care

Dr. Millner flexibly draws on therapies that have evidence of being effective, such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and related approaches (DBT, ACT, ERP).

Expert-Training

Dr. Millner received his PhD from Harvard University and clinically trained at Harvard Medical School, McLean Hospital, and MGH, all leaders in mental health care.

Goal-Oriented

Therapy entails working together to collaboratively develop personalized treatment plans and staying on track to meet therapy goals. For teenagers, this will likely include parents' input as well.

Treatment Areas

Dr. Millner treats teens (14+) and adults with mental health conditions that involve strong emotional reactions interfering with daily life
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Anxiety Disorders (Generalized, Social, Panic)
  • Mood Disorders (Depression, Bipolar)
  • Post-traumatic Stress
  • Stressful Life Transitions

Therapy Details

Insurance

Dr. Millner is not part of any insurance networks but will provide documentation for insurance reimbursement.

Frequency & Duration

For most clients, sessions are once a week for 50 min. However, the duration and frequency of sessions depend on clients' needs and preferences.

Initial Sessions

After a free consultation and an agreement to work together, initial sessions will focus on assessment to understand client symptoms and experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Although it can vary based on a client's needs and preferences, the goal is have 10-20 total sessions.
